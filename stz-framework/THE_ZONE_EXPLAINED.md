# The Zone — Plain Language

The Zone is where expert judgment and AI
capability meet exactly right.

## Below the Zone
The human is doing work the AI should be doing.
Manually reading documents. Reformatting notes.
Searching for information that already exists.
Expert time burned on execution work.

## Inside the Zone
The human is doing only what only they can do.
Making the call no algorithm can make.
Catching the signal no document contains.
Deciding what the output means for this person
in this moment.
Everything else is handled.

## Above the Zone
The human has handed too much to the AI.
Stopped interrogating outputs.
Stopped injecting judgment at the moments
that require it.
The expertise is leaking out.

## The Zone is not fixed
It moves as the AI gets smarter and as the
human gets better at encoding their expertise.
Every prompt refined, every skill defined,
every agent designed — you are widening the
Zone and moving the human deeper into it.
